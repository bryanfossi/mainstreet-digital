Full Transparency

A few things our prices don't cover.

Because we'd rather be upfront about it now than awkward about it later.

  • Ad spend Paid ads on Google, Meta, etc. are billed directly by the platform to you. We manage the campaigns; you keep ownership of the ad accounts.
  • Domain & hosting We help you set them up in your name (typically $15–$30/yr for the domain, $10–$25/mo for hosting). Both stay registered to you, not us.
  • Third-party tools Email platforms, scheduling tools, CRMs, stock photography — if your project needs them, we'll recommend the right fit and you'll subscribe directly.
  • Surprise fees There aren't any. Anything outside the scope of your package gets a quote up front before any work happens.

Pricing FAQ

Quick answers about what you'll pay.

Do I have to commit for a year?

No. Every monthly package is month-to-month with 30 days' notice to cancel. You stay because the work is paying off, not because of a contract clause.

What's the cheapest way to get started?

The Launch Site at $1,500 is the lowest-friction entry point if you don't have a website yet. If you already have one, the Social Starter at $497/mo gets you posting consistently. We'll talk through which makes sense on the consult.

Are ad spend and software included in your prices?

No — and that's intentional. Ad spend gets billed by Google or Meta directly to your account, and any third-party tools (email, CRM, scheduling, etc.) you subscribe to in your name. That way you own the accounts and can take them anywhere.

What happens if my needs change mid-package?

We adjust. Move up a tier, move down a tier, swap an add-on in or out — we'll quote the change up front and roll it in at your next billing cycle. No retroactive surprises.
