Common Questions

Things People Ask Us

Do you require long-term contracts?

No. All our packages are month-to-month. You stay because it's working, not because you're locked in. We earn your business every month — that's how we like it.

How much does a small business website cost?

Our websites start at $1,500 for a 5-page Launch Site delivered in 2–3 weeks. The Growth Site at $3,500 includes blog/CMS, lead capture, and SEO. Full-Service custom builds start at $6,500. Every package includes mobile-responsive design, 2+ revision rounds, and domain/hosting setup help.

What areas do you serve?

We work primarily with local businesses across Lancaster, Dauphin, Cumberland, and Berks Counties in Pennsylvania — and remotely with clients beyond that. The whole point of Main Street Digital is bringing big-agency quality to local Main Street businesses.

Who owns the website and accounts after you build them?

You do. Always. Your domain, your website code, your social media accounts, your Google Business Profile — they all belong to you. We build assets that are yours, full stop. If you ever leave us, you take everything with you.

How long does a new website take to build?

Launch Sites are delivered in 2–3 weeks. Growth Sites take 3–5 weeks. Full-Service custom builds run 5–8 weeks depending on scope. We give you a clear timeline up front in the proposal — and we hit our deadlines. If something comes up, you hear about it from us first.

Do I need both website design and social media management?

Not necessarily. Many of our clients start with one and add the other later. A website is your home base — every other channel points back to it. Social media keeps you top of mind for the customers you already have. The right starting point depends on where you're losing the most business right now. We'll help you figure it out on your free consultation.

Not sure which package is right?

Let's figure it out together. A free 30-minute call is all it takes to know exactly what your business needs — and what it doesn't.
